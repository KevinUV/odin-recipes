Chicken Carbonara

This pasta is a simple yet delicious chicken and shrimp carbonara.

It has pasta, creamy white sauce, chicken, shrimp, and a little bit of greens.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Chicken
  • Shrimp

Steps To make

  1. Boil Pasta
  2. Drain Pasta
  3. Cook chicken and shrimp
  4. Throw in sauce
  5. Blend in pan
  6. Serve with scallions
