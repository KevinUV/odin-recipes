Pizza

This pizza is a vegetable heavy that has lots of flavor.

It contains fresh dough, marinara sauce, parmesan cheese, olives, peppers, onions, and mushrooms.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Marinara
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Olives
  • Peppers
  • Onions
  • Mushrooms

Steps To make

  1. Get Dough and Bake it
  2. Add Marinara
  3. Add Cheese
  4. Throw in toppings
  5. Bake once again
  6. Slice and enjoy!
